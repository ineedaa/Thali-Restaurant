Our Story

Thali is an award winning restaurant in Dublin,Ireland ,renowned for producing simple food full of exotic flavours,using loacl,seasonal produce and traditional indian techniques.

The word "thali" refers to the way meals are eaten in India-where a selection of healthy , delicious and nutritiousdishes are served together on one plate

Thali showcases the hero dishes of the subcontinent from the back streets of Bombay to the beach shacks of Goa.
